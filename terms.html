1. Agreement

These Terms and Conditions ("Terms") govern the provision of driving instruction services by James Driving School ("we", "our", "us") to students ("you"). By booking or attending lessons, you agree to be bound by these Terms.

2. Eligibility

To enrol, you must:

  • Hold a valid Australian learner’s permit or licence relevant to the vehicle (or a recognized international licence with English translation).
  • Meet the legal driving age requirements.
  • Disclose any medical conditions, medications, or limitations affecting driving ability.
  • Present your physical licence at the start of every lesson. We reserve the right to refuse or suspend services if safety risks or licensing issues are identified.

3. Services

We provide:

  • Driving lessons (manual and automatic).
  • Driving test preparation and assessment readiness.
  • Use of an instructor vehicle for driving tests (subject to prior booking and instructor approval).

4. Bookings & Scheduling

  • Bookings are confirmed upon payment or deposit.
  • Start times are approximate; instructors may be delayed by up to 15 minutes due to traffic or prior lessons. We will endeavour to notify you of significant delays.
  • We reserve the right to reschedule lessons due to mechanical failure, instructor illness, or extreme weather.

5. Payment Terms & Tolls

  • All lessons must be paid in advance unless otherwise agreed.
  • We accept cash, card, and secure online payments.
  • Tolls: Unless otherwise stated, any road tolls (e.g., e-Toll) incurred during a lesson are the responsibility of the student and may be charged in addition to the lesson fee.

6. Cancellations & Rescheduling

  • 24-Hour Rule: A minimum of 24 hours’ notice is required to cancel or reschedule a lesson.
  • Late Cancellations: Cancellations made within 24 hours of the lesson will result in the forfeiture of the full lesson fee.
  • No-Shows: If you are not at the designated pickup location within 15 minutes of the start time, the instructor will depart and the full fee will be forfeited.

7. Refund Policy

  • Refunds are provided in accordance with the Australian Consumer Law.

8. Lesson Packages

9. Student Responsibilities & Conduct

  • You must follow all road rules and instructor directions immediately.
  • Zero Tolerance: You must not be under the influence of drugs or alcohol. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for aggressive, abusive, or disrespectful behaviour.
  • Safety: If an instructor deems your driving or behaviour to be a safety risk, the lesson will be terminated immediately without a refund.
